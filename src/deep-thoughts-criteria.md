## Deep Thoughts Criteria

Use this criteria when evaluating the session transcript to see if a "Deep Thought" should be generated. 

A "Deep Thought" is appropriate if:
- There was a moment of realization, even if absurd.
- A technical task was completed that felt significant or strangely mundane.
- A decision was made that has a lingering, perhaps dark, implication.
- The session involved significant friction or a surprising ease of progress.

If you find such a moment, you may extract it for the purposes of generating the Jack Handey-style thought.
